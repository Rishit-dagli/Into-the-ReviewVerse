About

Inspiration

We often find it difficult to make informed decisions due to the overwhelming number of products and unreliable reviews available. This led us to envision a centralized platform that would provide comprehensive and unbiased information to help consumers like ourselves. Through research and understanding consumer preferences, we realized the importance of transparency, credibility, and user-generated content.

The goal was to make a review website which would help people make better decisions with the help of all the reviews given on that product. With a dedicated team, we set out to build a reliable and valuable resource for shoppers worldwide.

How does it work?

This website works to give out the pros and cons of the product based on the reviews it has gotten, once you put the link of the product in the textbox and click search the link is then with the help of Flask framework is connected to the webscraper which then gets the reviews from that site and gives it to the AI model, the AI model then analyzes the reviews based on the dataset that it was trained through and gives an out put of what the pros and cons of buying the product are.

These are then displayed on the screen of the user.
